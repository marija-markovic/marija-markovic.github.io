About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Colorado Boulder, affiliated with the Department of Electrical, Computer, and Energy Engineering (Power Electronics & Renewable Energy Systems) and Renewable and Sustainable Energy Institute (RASEI), where I work under the supervision of Prof. Bri-Mathias Hodge. Prior to joining CU Boulder, I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Belgrade, Serbia, in 2016 and 2017, respectively. I was previously a Relay Protection & SCADA Engineer at the Serbian national transmission system operator (EMS) and a Project Engineer at Schneider Electric Hub.

My research mainly focuses on low-observable power systems. Specifically, my work explores data-driven methods for distribution modeling and sensing.

Research Interests

  • Power system operations
  • Renewable energy integration
  • Machine learning with applications in power and energy systems
